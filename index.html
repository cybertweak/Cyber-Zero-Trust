About Me

I'm Sal Tucker, an independent security architect with over 14 years of experience designing and hardening security infrastructure for Fortune 500 companies, high-growth startups, and government agencies.

My work sits at the intersection of risk management, cloud architecture, and secure software design. I don't just audit — I embed with engineering teams to build security in from the ground up, not bolt it on after the fact.

Whether you're migrating to cloud-native, implementing a zero-trust model, or need a threat model before your next product launch, I bring a structured, adversarial mindset to every engagement.
